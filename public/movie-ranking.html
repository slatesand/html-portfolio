My Favorite Movies of All Time

The Greatest Movies I Have Watched: Unranked

School of Rock

This is a classic comedy, accessible and enjoyable for all ages, but with an exceptional plot.

Penguins of Madagascar

Another classic comedy based off of the original Madagascar movie, focusing on the quartet of penguins. These guys are hilarious and have some ridiculous jokes.

InterStellar

A thought provoking, thrilling sci-fi movie that is entirely unpredictable. Amazing to watch for the first time, but arguably gets better with each subsequent watch.
